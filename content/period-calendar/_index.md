### The Simplest Way to Understand Your Body: Period Calendar

From period tracking and ovulation/fertility cycles to birth control reminders and health logging—we’ve built everything you need in one place.

### Key Features

- Accurate Cycle Tracking & Predictions
  Easily log your dates and get smarter predictions over time. Know exactly when your next period, ovulation, and fertile window will be.

- Personal Health Reports
  See your average cycle length and trends at a glance. Understanding your body's rhythm has never been easier.

- Daily Symptom Logging
  Keep track of your moods, pain, and physical symptoms. Review your history anytime to stay in tune with your cycle.

- Smart Reminders Just for You
  Stay prepared with alerts for your upcoming period and fertile days.

- Private & Secure
  Your data is sensitive and personal. Protect it with a PIN or fingerprint lock for total peace of mind.

### This app is perfect if you:

- Want a reliable way to manage irregular cycles.

- Need to know your ovulation and fertile days for pregnancy or contraception.
