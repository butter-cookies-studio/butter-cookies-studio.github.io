Keep your plants happy and thriving with Plant Care: Watering & Diary! 🌱✨

Are you a passionate plant parent who sometimes forgets when to water? Or do you want to capture the beautiful moments of your green friends sprouting new leaves? We are here to help you become the perfect plant butler!

With a simple, cozy interface, managing your home garden has never been easier. Focus on the joy of growing, and let us handle the scheduling.

🎨 Key Features:

🌿 1. All Your Plants at a Glance
• Check your personalized plant list with essential details.
• Instantly access growth logs, record watering events, or edit/delete plant profiles with a single tap.

💧 2. Smart Watering Reminders & Push Notifications
• Set a custom watering cycle for each plant (choose intervals in days, start date, and preferred notification time).
• Receive gentle push notifications so you never miss a watering day again.
• Keep your plants healthy with a precise tracking system.

📸 3. Beautiful Plant Growth Diary
• Track your plant’s journey over time with a dedicated timeline feed.
• See how many days you’ve grown together with an affectionate D-Day counter.
• Add lovely photos and quick, heartfelt notes to remember every milestone—like new sprouts, pruning, or repotting.

🏡 4. Effortless Plant Onboarding & Editing
• Easily register new plants or update existing ones.
• Customize everything: choose the plant species, give it a cute nickname, upload a profile picture, and assign it to its favorite spot in your home (Living Room, Balcony, Bedroom, Kitchen, or Office).
