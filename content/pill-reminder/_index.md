### The simplest way to track your medications.

Keep your health on track with our intuitive pill reminder app.

### Key Features:

- Weekly Calendar: View and log your daily medication schedule at a glance.

- Quick Add: Choose from a curated list of popular meds and vitamins for easy setup.

- Flexible Routines: Supports daily, specific days, or interval (X days) schedules.

- Custom Dosages: Set multiple time slots and adjust dosages for each time of day.

- Medication List: Manage all your current prescriptions and supplements in one place.

Stay healthy and never miss your pills again!
